Interstate Train Lines

Melbourne to/from Adelaide (via Bendigo and Nhill/Horsham)

Train from Southern Cross Station (Melbourne) to Bendigo (change) then, connecting coach service to Adelaide via Horsham or Nhill.

Return journey from Adelaide to Melbourne can also be booked.

Melbourne to/from Canberra (via Albury)

CanberraLink

Train from Melbourne to Albury via Seymour (change) then, coach from Seymour to Canberra via Albury.

Return journey from Canberra to Melbourne can also be booked.

Melbourne to/from Canberra (via Bairnsdale)

Capital Link

Return journey from Canberra to Melbourne can also be booked.

Mildura to/from Albury

Coach from Mildura to Swan Hill (change) then, connecting train from Swan Hill to Kerang (change) then, connecting coach to Albury.

Return journey from Mildura to Albury can also be booked.
